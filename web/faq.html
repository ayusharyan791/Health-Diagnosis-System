Frequently Asked Questions



Q1. What is an AI-powered chatbot, and how does it work on this website?

Ans: An AI-powered chatbot is a computer program that uses artificial intelligence to simulate human conversation. It's designed to provide information, assist with tasks, and enhance your overall website experience.

Ans: Yes, our chatbot is available 24/7, ensuring you can get assistance and answers to your questions anytime, day or night.
